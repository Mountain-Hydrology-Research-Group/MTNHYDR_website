Postdoctoral Scholars

We very seldom have funding to hire postdoctoral researchers. However, if you are interested in developing a research proposal and securing your own funding to join the group, please read on. Some of the links below are for Postdoctoral Opportunities in locations other than the University of Washington. I encourage you to apply multiple places. The Joint Institute for the Study of the Atmosphere and Ocean (JISAO) at the University of Washington often has competitive funding for people interested in interdisciplinary climate research, with changing deadlines each year. If you are interested in this or another opportunity, please contact Professor Jessica Lundquist (jdlund@uw.edu). In your e-mail, please specify your specific interests (including some research ideas), your prior publications, and your prior experience.
