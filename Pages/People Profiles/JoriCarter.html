Undergraduate Student

Jori is from the Seattle area and is an undergraduate in civil engineering. He is interested in hydrology and working with natural systems. After learning how to code in high school, he quickly became very interested in how technology could change and improve the way that we design and engineer our infrastructure, making it sustainable and safe for the environment.

Besides helping with research, Jori is the lab's web developer. He is in charge of managing the sites and redesigning them.
